## Monetary System

### Early Period
- **Aes Grave** - Heavy bronze chunks (literally called "heavy money")
- **As** - The workhorse bronze coin that paid for everything

### Imperial Period
- **Aureus** - The flex coin in gold
- **Denarius** - Silver coin that literally paid the army
- **Sestertius** - Bronze/orichalcum hybrid, surprisingly rare in nice condition today

### Later Developments
- **Caracalla's Reforms (215 CE)** - Sneaky debasement alert! The new antoninianus promised two denarii's worth but delivered way less silver
- **Constantine's Makeover** - Ditched the aureus, welcomed the solidus. This gold coin was so good it outlived the entire empire!
